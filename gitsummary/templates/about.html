About GitSummary

Gitsummary is an application that i thought about to allow users or potential users of github use to view their favourite github user's profile or their favourite repsoitories
Gitsummary is an easy to use github profile and repository search engine for those who do not want to go through the trouble of navigating through github or for those who do not want the hassle of leaving multiple tabs of multiple repsoitories open on thier browsers.
Gitsummary gives it's users the ability to save their favourite repositories for later. Of course users can also delete repositories that they feel that they do not need anymore.
